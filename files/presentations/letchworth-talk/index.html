A little story about a planet that could.

  • Formed around a boring single star
  • A rocky planet - a surface to walk on
  • Was just the right distance away from its star
  • No rogue planets, no disruptions, no supernovae
  • Survived a collision that broke off a chunk
  • Water and life.

Of course this planet is our Earth.

  • we have very few planets as good models
  • so we turn to planets outside out solar system
  • first discovered in 1995
